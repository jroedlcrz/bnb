Enagic Golf Course/Holes Layout

Enagic Golf Club was designed by world-renowned architect Ted Robinson. This 18-hole, par 72 Championship Course plays over 6800 yards from the PGA Tees with four sets of tees, Bent Grass Greens and Bermuda Grass with Over-seeded Rye Grass Fairways. Enagic Golf Club features six lakes and three waterfalls to highlight a perfect setting for your golfing experience.
